Professional Profile

I come from technical support, hospitality operations and remote customer service, and I am now building a path in software development.

I have lived and worked in Portugal, England, Greece and Brazil. That experience made me comfortable working with different people, incomplete information and changing priorities.

In support roles, small details mattered. A missed instruction, an unclear message or an incomplete record could create real problems for the person on the other side. That is why I pay attention to process, context, documentation and follow-through.

Today, while studying Systems Analysis and Development and building practical projects, I try to bring the same mindset into technology: understand the problem, organize the information, build with purpose and keep the user experience clear.

Currently learning
I’m currently improving my front-end skills through responsive interfaces, JavaScript practice, accessibility basics, Git/GitHub workflows and project pages based on real service routines.
Interests
